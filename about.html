What is CIunit.org?

The Conceptual Investigations Unit is a home for careful inquiry.

CIunit.org is the public-facing site for a research consortium focused on developing clearer ways to think about complex scientific, technological, and societal questions.

Why “conceptual investigations”?

Many important disagreements are not only about facts, but about framing: what question is being asked, what is being held fixed, what counts as success, and how different values enter the analysis. CIunit aims to make those structures explicit.
